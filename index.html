How it works

The Solana blockchain charges rent for unused SPL token accounts. Closing empty accounts refunds rent back to your wallet. Each empty token account typically releases 0.00204 SOL when closed.

FAQ

What is account rent? SPL token accounts require rent to remain allocated. If token balance is zero, you can close the account and reclaim rent to your wallet.

Is it safe? You only close accounts you own and that are empty. Refunds come from deallocation of the account.

Fees and donations? Optional 5% donation. If a referrer is present via ?ref= or ?summoner=, a share goes to them. User pays gas for their transactions.
